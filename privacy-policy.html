Privacy Policy for Quick Notes Pro

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

Quick Notes Pro respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how Quick Notes Pro handles user data when using the browser extension.

Information We Collect

Quick Notes Pro does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information.

The extension operates entirely on your local device. Any notes, reminders, tasks, ideas, or other content entered into Quick Notes Pro are stored locally within your browser using browser storage functionality.

How We Use Information

The information you enter into Quick Notes Pro is used solely to provide note-taking functionality.

  • Save notes locally on your device
  • Load previously saved notes automatically
  • Allow editing and deleting notes
  • Maintain note data between browser sessions

No note content is transmitted to any external server.

Data Sharing

Quick Notes Pro does not:

  • Sell user data
  • Share user data with third parties
  • Transfer user data to external services
  • Use advertising trackers
  • Use analytics tools

Data Security

Since all notes remain on your device, you retain control over your information. Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their devices and browser profiles.

Permissions

Quick Notes Pro only requests permissions necessary to provide its functionality.

  • Storage permission for saving notes locally
  • Extension interface access for note management

The extension does not access:

  • Browsing history
  • Website content
  • User accounts
  • Location information
  • Contacts
  • Personal files

Children's Privacy

Quick Notes Pro is not intended for children under the age of 13. The extension does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the project's GitHub repository.
