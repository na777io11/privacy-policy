Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Quick Notes Pro is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how user data is handled when using the extension.

Information Collection

Quick Notes Pro does not collect personally identifiable information and does not require users to create an account.

The extension does not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location information
  • Payment information
  • Browsing history
  • Personal identifiers

Local Storage Usage

Quick Notes Pro uses the Chrome Storage API solely to save notes created by the user. These notes are stored locally within the browser environment and remain under the user's control.

No note content is transmitted to external servers.

Data Sharing

Quick Notes Pro does not sell, rent, share, transfer, or disclose any user information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The extension does not use analytics services, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection systems.

Permissions

Quick Notes Pro requests the following permission:

  • storage — Used exclusively for saving and retrieving user notes locally.

Security

All stored information remains within the user's browser. Users can delete their notes at any time using the extension's built-in functionality.

Children's Privacy

Quick Notes Pro is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page along with an updated effective date.

Contact Information

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, users may contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store support channel.
